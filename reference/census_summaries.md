# Summarize Census Block Group Demographics

These helpers aggregate American Community Survey (ACS) estimates
returned by
[`mysterycall_get_census_data()`](https://mufflyt.github.io/mysterycall/reference/mysterycall_get_census_data.md)
to produce tidy summary tables and visualizations. They focus on the
sex-by-age table (B01001) so that analysts can quickly spot the
distribution of female populations across block groups or higher-level
geographies.
